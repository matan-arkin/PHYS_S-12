About

Let’s face it, you’re here to read about the PROJECT, not me. And why wouldn’t you? The project is AWESOME. So whether you accidentally wandered over here, or you’re an instructor who will now grade this page, I must direct you to where the real magic happens: the final project proposal page.

Then about me: hello! My name is Matan, 22, from Israel, and I’m a Harvard Extension School student majoring in CS (rising senior), a recently discharged soldier from the Israel Defense Forces where I was a cyber researcher and now employed as an embedded developer at a wicked-cool augmented reality startup called Magic Leap! For fun, I enjoy Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, SCUBA diving and basically any other water/snow sport.

When I completed my military service 9 months ago, I realized cyber research wasn’t quite my thing and that I had a knack for TANGIBLE work. I like seeing things move and interact with the real world. So I dropped everything, locked myself up in a room for 3 months (it was COVID, I’m not a loner), and learned embedded programing on my own as best I could. I read books, bought hardware, wrote a driver, prepared for interview questions – all that good stuff. I found my dream job at Magic Leap. And I might’ve found just the course to go along with it.

If you stayed with me through all this, I’m deeply humbled :)
